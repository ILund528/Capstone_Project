Umbralla Hand

We started as a group of people who wanted to help others. So we banded together and created a little place called UmbrallHand and started out by placing fylers around the local areas but that was not enough. So we wait till we can got enough donations then we bought a website addreess and created this site so we can branch ourselves further. Now we cover over 30% of Arizona and still growing, we are a non-profit organization who will accept donation to our cause. We built this so we can help make a difference in people's lives and hope they do the same for others near them. So please join our cause to help others in need.
