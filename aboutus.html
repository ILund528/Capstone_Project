Umbralla Hand

We started as a group of people who wanted to help others. So we banded together and created a little place called Umbrall Hand and started out by placing flyers around the local areas but that was not enough. So we saved up till we can got enough money, then we bought a website addreess and created this site so we can branch ourselves further and reach more more people to help us or help others by volunteering. Now we have 5 shelters across Arizona and still growing, we are a non-profit organization who will accept donation to our cause. We built this so, we can help make a difference in people's lives and end economic differentiation. So please join our cause to help others in need and to better is community.
