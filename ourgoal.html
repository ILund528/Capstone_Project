Umbralla Hand

Our goals is to create a bridge between the economic classes in attempt to end economic differentiation. We also would like to bring the homeless up from the socio-economic position they are in. Here are some things we will provide to the homeless.

  • Food
  • water
  • Clothes
  • pillows
  • blankets
  • Shelter
  • Education

We do this in hope that they can get on their feet and have an actually life. So please if you want to help us go to the volunteers page or if you want to get in contact with us go to the about us page.
